David J. Graham Banman

M.Ed. (Ed. Psych.), CCC, Registered Psychologist

David has worked as a counsellor for 25 years. He has worked with adolescents, couples and individuals who are experiencing challenges.

He holds a Master of Education (Ed. Psych.), is a Registered Psychologist and a Canadian Certified Counsellor.

David completed the Solution-Focused Counselling Certificate Program at the University of Toronto.
